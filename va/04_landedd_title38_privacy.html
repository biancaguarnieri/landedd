Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026 · Plain language, accurate to how the product actually works.

The short version

We do not store your resume or job description content. When you use a free tool, your inputs are sent to our AI provider, processed, and discarded. Nothing is written to a database.

For Premium purchases, we retain your email address, order record, and the AI-generated analysis we delivered to you, because we are legally required to keep transaction records. We do not retain the underlying resume or JOA content beyond the time needed to generate and deliver your analysis.

We do not sell data, run advertising trackers, or use your content to train AI models.

1. Who we are

This Privacy Policy describes how Landedd LLC ("Landedd," "we," "us") handles personal information in connection with the Landedd Title 38 product. Our contact email is hello@landedd.com; mailing address is listed at the end.

2. What we collect

2.1 Information you provide to free tools

When you use a free tool (such as the JOA Tier Classifier or Questionnaire Strategy Guide), you may provide text inputs — a resume, a job description, a USAJobs URL, or answers to questions. This content is transmitted to our AI provider for processing and is not stored in our systems. After your tool session ends, this content is no longer accessible to us or the AI provider in any retrievable form.

2.2 Information you provide for the Premium analysis

When you purchase the Premium analysis, you provide:

  • Email address (required, for delivery and customer service)
  • Payment information (handled directly by Stripe; we do not see or store full card numbers)
  • Intake form responses (credential level, licenses, certifications, years of experience, geographic preference, etc.)
  • Resume content and target JOA URLs or text

The AI-generated Premium analysis is generated from these inputs and delivered to your email. The intake responses, resume content, and JOA content used to generate your analysis are processed in memory and discarded after delivery. The analysis output itself, your email, your order record, and your one follow-up question (if used) are retained as described in Section 4.

2.3 Optional feedback

If you submit feedback through a feedback form, we collect your message, optional email address, optional star rating, and which tool you used. This is stored so we can improve the product and follow up with you if you asked us to.

2.4 Automatic data

We use standard server logs that may capture IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited. These are retained for up to 30 days for security, debugging, and basic analytics. Aggregated, non-identifying usage statistics may be retained longer for product improvement.

We do not use third-party advertising trackers or behavioral profiling cookies. The only browser storage we use is localStorage for tracking your free-tool usage limits in your own browser; this data never leaves your device.

3. What we do not collect

  • We do not require you to create an account to use free tools.
  • We do not collect Social Security Numbers, dates of birth (unless voluntarily included in a resume), or government identification numbers.
  • We do not collect medical or health information beyond what you voluntarily include in your resume content.
  • We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 18.

4. How we use and retain information

4.1 To deliver the Service

Inputs you provide are used to generate the Service's outputs (free-tool results and the Premium analysis).

4.2 Retention

  • Free-tool inputs: Not stored. Discarded after the AI call returns.
  • Premium intake content (resume, JOA content, intake responses): Not stored after analysis delivery.
  • Premium delivered analysis output and order record: Retained for 7 years to comply with U.S. tax and business-records requirements.
  • Email address (Premium customers): Retained while needed for delivery, follow-up question, customer service, and tax/audit purposes.
  • Feedback submissions: Retained until we have completed any product improvement or follow-up communication you requested.
  • Server logs: Retained up to 30 days.

4.3 What we do not do

We do not use your content to train AI models. We do not sell, lease, or trade your data to third parties for commercial purposes. We do not run targeted advertising based on your content.

5. Third-party service providers

To deliver the Service, we use the following providers, each under contractual data-protection terms:

  • Anthropic — provides the Claude AI used to process your inputs and generate outputs. Anthropic's processing of API content is governed by their published commercial terms and privacy policy. We do not opt into AI training on your content.
  • Stripe — processes Premium payments. Stripe receives your payment information directly; we do not store card numbers.
  • Vercel — provides hosting infrastructure. Vercel may process standard request data as part of serving the site.
  • Resend — provides transactional email delivery for Premium analyses and customer service emails.
  • Redis (managed by our hosting provider) — stores aggregated usage metrics and rate-limit counters. We do not store resume or JOA content here.

We do not send your data to any AI provider other than Anthropic.

6. Disclosure of information

We may disclose information when required by law (subpoena, court order, government request) or to protect our legal rights. We do not voluntarily disclose information to third parties for any other purpose.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal information:

7.1 Rights under U.S. state privacy laws

If you are a resident of California, Colorado, Connecticut, Delaware, Indiana, Iowa, Kentucky, Maryland, Minnesota, Montana, Nebraska, New Hampshire, New Jersey, Oregon, Rhode Island, Tennessee, Texas, Utah, or Virginia, you may have rights including:

  • The right to know what personal information we hold about you
  • The right to request a copy of your personal information in a portable format
  • The right to request correction or deletion
  • The right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (we do not sell or share)
  • The right to opt out of profiling for decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects (we do not engage in such profiling)
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@landedd.com from the email address associated with your account or order. We will respond within the timeframes required by applicable law (typically 30–45 days). We will verify your identity before fulfilling deletion or access requests.

Note on resume content: Because we do not store resume content, there is generally no resume data to delete. Deletion requests will cover your email address, order records (subject to legal retention obligations), feedback submissions, and the delivered analysis output.

7.2 Rights under GDPR (EU/UK residents)

If you are in the EU, UK, or another GDPR-aligned jurisdiction, you have the rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection. Email hello@landedd.com to exercise these rights. The Service is operated from the United States; by using the Service, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in the United States.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at rest where applicable. The Service's "no resume storage" architecture is itself a primary security control: data we never store cannot be breached. However, no system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of any data transmitted over the internet.

9. Children

The Service is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, contact us at hello@landedd.com and we will delete it.

10. International users

The Service is operated from the United States. If you access the Service from outside the U.S., your information is transferred to and processed in the U.S., which may have different data-protection laws than your home jurisdiction.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent change. Material changes will be communicated via the Service or by email to Premium customers.

12. Contact
