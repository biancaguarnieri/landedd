Frequently asked questions

Is Landedd really free?

Yes — during beta, all tools are free to use. No credit card, no sign-up required. We may introduce paid plans in the future for power users.

Will you store my resume?

No. Your resume and job descriptions are processed in memory and not stored. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

The tool gave me inaccurate output — what do I do?

AI isn't perfect. Always review the generated content before submitting it to an employer. If something seems off, try rephrasing your input or adding more detail. And let us know via the feedback form — it helps us improve.

Can I use Landedd for multiple job applications?

Absolutely — that's what it's built for. Tailor your resume to each role for the best results.
